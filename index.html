About Me

Hello! My name is Pantavis Tantivongsakij, but you can call me Beam. I'm thrilled to welcome you to my corner of the internet. As a proud native of Bangkok, Thailand, I've spent all of my 16 years in this vibrant and bustling city.

Growing up in a large family of five, including two older sisters, 'peaceful' was never a term could describe the atmosphere. Nevertheless, I always found time for my Video games, an activity that would eventually spark my curiosity and passion for the amazing world of technology. As I delved deeper into this magical realm, I found myself drawn to the constructs and blueprints that built up what was on my screen! Ever since then I have set a goal of becoming an aspiring Computer Engineer in the future. Outside of school, during my free times, I like to spend it engaging in various sports, from football to volleyball - you name it, I'll play it.

Through this website, I aim to share a glimpse into my world and life as well as my experiences and journey so far in the world of Computer Science. Please feel free to explore and enjoy your time on this website! :)
